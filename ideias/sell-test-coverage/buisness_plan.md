# Business Plan — Test Coverage as a Service

## Executive Summary

We provide comprehensive **test coverage solutions** for software teams — spanning backend applications, CLI tools, and front-end web applications. Through a centralized platform, clients can visualize, generate, manage, and maintain their test suites with full transparency and control.

## Value Proposition

Most engineering teams understand the importance of testing but lack the time, expertise, or tooling to achieve meaningful coverage. We bridge that gap by offering:

- A **unified platform** for test visibility, generation, and reporting.
- **Flexible service tiers** that scale from self-serve tooling to fully managed test engineering.
- Expertise across the entire stack — backend, CLI, and front-end.

## Target Market

- Small-to-medium software companies without dedicated QA teams.
- Startups looking to establish testing culture early without heavy hiring.
- Enterprises seeking to augment their existing QA capacity for specific projects or sprints.

## Service Tiers

| Tier | Description | Ideal For |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **Platform Access** | The client company gets full access to the platform to create, run, and monitor their own tests. | Teams with in-house QA who need better tooling. |
| **Platform + Test Construction** | Platform access plus our team builds the entire test suite for the client's codebase. | Teams that need coverage fast but can maintain it themselves. |
| **Platform + Test Construction + Maintenance** | Full-service engagement — we build, monitor, and continuously maintain the test suite as the codebase evolves. | Teams that want a hands-off, fully managed testing solution. |

## Competitive Advantages

- **Stack-agnostic coverage** — backend, CLI, and web front-end under one roof.
- **Transparent reporting** — clients always see exactly what is tested, what is not, and why.
- **Scalable engagement model** — clients can start with platform-only and upgrade to managed services as their needs grow.
